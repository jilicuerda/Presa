Presa Identity

Who Are We?

At the heart of the competitive Valorant scene, PRESA was born—a Spanish-speaking esports club founded on the pillars of hard work, seriousness, and pure ambition.

pets The Spirit of the Presa Canario

Our name isn't a coincidence. We draw our inspiration from the Presa Canario, a unique dog breed famous for its imposing strength and protective nature. We represent the constant struggle. We come from the bottom, without the massive budgets of industry giants, but with an insatiable hunger to compete, fight hard on the server, and do things the right way. Where others throw money, we bring grit, heart, and strategy.

psychology Our Philosophy

We firmly believe in the potential of our region. Because of this, our philosophy is clear: always compete with a 100% Spanish-speaking roster and open doors for real growth. We don't care about the rank you start with; we care about where you want to go. Talent alone opens doors, but only discipline, consistency, and daily effort build the true champions of tomorrow. We give opportunities to those who prove they have the drive to become the best.
